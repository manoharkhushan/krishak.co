Government Agricultural Schemes for Farmers

Explore various government schemes designed to support farmers and improve agricultural productivity.

PM-KISAN
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

Benefits: ₹6,000 per year directly to farmers' bank accounts in three installments of ₹2,000 each.

Eligibility: All landholding farmer families (subject to certain exclusions).

How to Apply: Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or apply online at pmkisan.gov.in

Official Website
Kisan Credit Card
KCC Scheme

Benefits: Credit limit up to ₹3 lakh at concessional interest rates (4% p.a.), flexible repayment options.

Eligibility: All farmers - individual/joint borrowers who are owner cultivators, tenant farmers, oral lessees.

How to Apply: Apply at any bank branch or through bank official website.

Official Website
PMFBY
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Benefits: Low premium (2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops), full sum insured for crop loss due to natural calamities.

Eligibility: All farmers including sharecroppers and tenant farmers.

How to Apply: Through bank branch, CSC, or official PMFBY portal.

Official Website
Soil Health Card
National Soil Health Card Mission

Benefits: Free soil testing, personalized fertilizer recommendations based on soil nutrient status.

Eligibility: All farmers across the country.

How to Apply: Visit nearest soil testing laboratory or apply through state agricultural department.

Official Website
PMKSY
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Benefits: Financial assistance for irrigation infrastructure, drip irrigation, sprinkler systems, water harvesting structures.

Eligibility: All farmers, especially those in water-scarce regions.

How to Apply: Apply through state agriculture department or district agricultural office.

Official Website
e-NAM
Electronic National Agriculture Market

Benefits: Online trading platform, transparent price discovery, single license across multiple mandis, real-time price information.

Eligibility: Farmers, traders, and commission agents registered with APMC mandis.

How to Apply: Register at eNAM portal or through local APMC mandi.

Official Website
PKVY
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Benefits: Financial assistance for organic farming, ₹20,000 per hectare for 3 years, certification support.

Eligibility: Farmer groups, FPOs, or individual farmers interested in organic farming.

How to Apply: Apply through state agricultural department or District Agricultural Officer.

Official Website
SMAM
Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

Benefits: 35-50% subsidy on agricultural machinery, custom hiring centers, training for farmers.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers get higher subsidy, also available for custom hiring centers.

How to Apply: Through state agricultural engineering department or district agricultural office.

Official Website
NMSA
National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture

Benefits: Support for climate-resilient farming, water use efficiency, organic farming, crop diversification.

Eligibility: Farmers practicing or interested in sustainable agriculture practices.

How to Apply: Through state agricultural department or District Agricultural Officer.

Official Website
PM-AASHA
Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan

Benefits: Minimum Support Price (MSP) for pulses, oilseeds, and copra, procurement through government agencies.

Eligibility: Farmers growing pulses, oilseeds, and copra.

How to Apply: Through local procurement center or state agricultural department.

Official Website
RKVY-RAFTAAR
Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana

Benefits: Financial assistance for agricultural development projects, infrastructure, value chain development.

Eligibility: State governments, implementing agencies, farmer groups.

How to Apply: Through state agricultural department or district agricultural office.

Official Website
Kisan Call Centre
Agricultural Helpline Service

Benefits: Free agricultural advice in local languages, expert guidance on farming techniques, pest management, weather information.

Eligibility: All farmers across India.

How to Apply: Call toll-free number 1800-XXX-XXXX (state-wise numbers available).

Official Website
Important Information
  • Schemes and benefits may vary by state. Please check with your local agricultural department for state-specific details.
  • Always verify eligibility criteria before applying for any scheme.
  • Documents like Aadhaar card, land records, and bank account details are generally required for most schemes.
  • For more information, visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK).
